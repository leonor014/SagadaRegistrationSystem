About Sagada

Sagada is a serene town located in the province of Mountain Province, Philippines. Known for its stunning landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and adventure-filled activities, it has become a favorite destination for travelers seeking tranquility and natural beauty. From exploring the ancient hanging coffins to trekking through the lush mountains and witnessing breathtaking sunrises at Pog-o Viewpoint, Marlboro and Langsayan. Sagada offers a unique experience that resonates with nature and history lovers alike.
